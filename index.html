Project: 30MFF Video

This video was recorded in and around the arts center (building C3) located in New York University Abu Dhabi. The arts center is the building where performers and artists from all over the world come to display their talents. The movie attempts to capture the inherent structures, frameworks and patterns that lay the foundation of all the free and abstract expression of art going on inside the building. The scenes were filmed inside, and in the vicinity of the building, around daytime, when the arts center is the least busiest.
